Methodology

A structured path
to reliable findings.

Every engagement follows a consistent, transparent methodology. From initial instruction through final reporting, we maintain complete independence and systematic rigour at each stage.

Step 01

Instruction & Scope

We receive and carefully review instructions, establishing scope, timeline, and access requirements before any field activity begins.

Step 02

Field Investigation

On-site inspection and evidence collection conducted according to established survey protocols, with meticulous contemporaneous records.

Step 03

Technical Analysis

Findings are subjected to rigorous technical analysis, referencing applicable standards, regulations, and established marine practice.

Step 04

Reporting & Advisory

Comprehensive written reports delivered with clarity and precision, structured for use by legal, commercial, and technical audiences alike.
